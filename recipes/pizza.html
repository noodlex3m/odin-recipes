Pizza

Description

Pizza is a savory dish made with a round, flat base of dough topped with sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add the mozzarella cheese and other toppings.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  6. Cut and serve hot.
